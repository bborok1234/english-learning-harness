# Code of Conduct

This project exists to help people practice English with less fear and more consistency. Contributions should protect that tone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume language learners may feel exposed or anxious.
- Prefer clear examples over sarcasm or dismissive comments.
- Respect privacy around learner transcripts, voice notes, images, and local practice data.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Sexualized, hateful, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing another person's private learner data without consent.
- Repeatedly derailing issues or pull requests after maintainers ask to stop.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block users; or take other reasonable action to keep the project safe and useful.

Report concerns through the security/contact path in `SECURITY.md`.
